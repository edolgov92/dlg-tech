### Project Context: Boutique Engineering Collective (Managed by Senior Architect)

**1. Project Overview**
We are building a high-end portfolio/landing page for a **boutique software engineering collective** led by Eugene Dolgov (DLG Tech), a Senior Solutions Architect with **14+ years of experience** (US/Europe background).
The business model is **"Managed Team Extension"**: clients get the architectural expertise and communication skills of a US-based Tech Lead (Eugene), while the execution is handled by his vetted team of Senior/Middle+ engineers from Eastern Europe.

**2. Core Value Proposition (The "Hook")**
*   **Problem:** US Founders struggle to find high-quality engineering talent. Local hires are too expensive ($150k+), and traditional outsourcing agencies often suffer from poor code quality and communication barriers.
*   **Our Solution:** The "Proxy Model." The client deals *only* with Eugene (perfect English, US time zone overlap, Architect level). Eugene translates business requirements into technical specs, manages the non-English speaking dev team, performs 100% code review, and delivers enterprise-grade results.
*   **Benefit:** Silicon Valley quality architecture at rational offshore rates ($45-50/hr).

**3. Target Audience**
*   Non-technical Founders of funded startups (Seed/Series A).
*   Digital Agencies needing white-label technical back-end.
*   CTOs looking for a reliable "fire-and-forget" dev squad.

**4. Design Direction & Vibe**
*   **Style:** "Interactive Engineering," "Dark Mode," "Glassmorphism."
*   **Aesthetic:** Minimalist but deeply technical. High-end typography (e.g., Inter, Satoshi, Clash Display).
*   **Key Feeling:** Reliability, Speed, Intelligence. The site should feel like a well-engineered tool, not just a brochure.
*   **References:** Linear.app, Vercel, Rauno.me, Diagram.com.
*   **Interactivity:** Subtle micro-interactions, code-syntax visuals, smooth scroll animations (Framer Motion).

**5. Site Structure & Content Strategy**

*   **Hero Section:**
    *   *Headline:* "Senior Architect-Led Engineering Team."
    *   *Sub:* "14 years of experience. US standards. Managed execution. I handle the architecture; my team handles the code."
    *   *Visual:* High-quality photo of Eugene or an interactive 3D/Code visualization.
    *   *CTA:* "Book Intro Call."

*   **Trust Battery:**
    *   Logos of past companies Eugene worked with (US/EU tech giants/startups).

*   **The Model (How it Works):**
    *   Step 1: **Architecture & Oversight:** Eugene designs the system.
    *   Step 2: **Deep Work:** Vetted Senior Engineers build the core (Silent execution).
    *   Step 3: **Quality Gate:** Evgeny reviews every PR before it hits production.

*   **Tech Stack Display:**
    *   Modern visualization of the stack: React, Node.js, Python, AWS, AI/LLM integration.

*   **The Team (Humanizing):**
    *   Candid/Professional photos of the dev squad.
    *   Focus on stats: "8 Years Exp," "Ex-FinTech," etc.

**6. Technical Implementation Goals**
*   **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) + TypeScript.
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS.
*   **Animation:** Framer Motion (for scroll reveals and micro-interactions).
*   **Performance:** 100/100 Lighthouse score is mandatory.

**7. Key Constraints for AI Generation**
*   **Copywriting:** Must be confident, concise, and professional. Avoid "salesy" agency fluff (e.g., avoid "We are a passionate team of dreamers"). Use "I" when Eugene speaks, "We" when referring to execution.
*   **Focus:** Emphasize that Eugene is the *guarantor* of quality. The client is buying *peace of mind*.
